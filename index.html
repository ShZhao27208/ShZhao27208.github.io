Shuo Zhao 赵 烁 / 瞾

Zhao Shuo, male, is from Xi'an, Shaanxi Province. He is a 2022 undergraduate student majoring in Materials Science and Engineering at Chongqing Jiaotong University. In August 2023, he joined Professor Wang Feng's research group, focusing on inorganic long afterglow materials. He is proficient in operating equipment such as XRD and fluorescence spectrometers, as well as scientific research software like Origin and Materials Studio. The research projects he has participated in include: Sr₁.₅Ca₀.₅Al₂SiO₇:aCe⁴⁺,Tb³⁺ (published in CrystEngComm, first author, 1/9); BaCa₂Y₆O₁₂:Sm³⁺ (published in Ceramics International, co-first author second, 2/7); CaY₂Ge₄O₁₂ : Eu³⁺ (published in CrystEngComm, co-first author second, 2/7); YAlGeO₅: Sm³⁺ (published in Ceramics International, third author, 3/10). He is also a core member of the municipal-level innovation and entrepreneurship project "Regulation of Vanadium-based Multicomponent Metal-Organic Frameworks in Nanoscale Confinement and Research on Energy Storage of Aqueous Zinc-Ion Batteries" (ranked second). He has won five national-level and other competition awards and three university-level honors and scholarships. He served as a student assistant for the metallographic competition, coordinating events for over 200 participants, and was the class study committee member for three years and the calligraphy club president for one year. He has a solid professional foundation and strong self-study ability, can adapt to high-intensity working environments, and has good teamwork and communication skills.
